Tournament Rules

  • This is a 1v1 double elimination tournament.

  • The Players MUST race using the same seed.

  • The seed MUST be provided by one of the tournament Referees.

  • The seed MUST be applied using the “Safe” preset.

  • The Players MUST NOT have played the seed prior to their match.

  • The Players SHOULD confirm the seed is correct when it is displayed in-game during the File Select screen’s info text.

  • The Players SHOULD choose the “Show spoilers” option when patching their game so that they are aware of their starting equipment in order to confirm they are playing the correct seed if they failed to confirm on the File Select screen.

  • The Players MUST NOT choose the “Show relic locations” option when patching their game.

  • All seeds MUST be completed in Replay Mode. The Players MUST NOT start their game in Axe Armor, Luck, or Richter mode.

  • The Players MUST NOT watch the stream of their match, or be in communication with any non-Referee watching their opponent's stream, or in any other way obtain information on the seed or their opponent's route.

  • The Players MUST observe SRL timing for their runs:

    • Timing begins when a Player presses Start on the Name Entry screen.

    • Timing ends when the Player lands their last hit on Dracula’s true form.

  • The Players MUST observe the rules of the “Glitchless + Spike Breaker” category (GSB):

    • The Players MAY use holy water damage stacks on Dracula during the prologue.

    • The Players MUST NOT skip Death cutscene using any glitches such as RSL.

    • The Players MAY skip Death cutscene using a Library Card if they received one as a random enemy drop, in a breakable wall, or, as a prologue reward.

    • The Players MAY use untransform mechanics to reach otherwise restricted areas. This includes, for instance, dive-kicking the candle under the clock room entrance to Olrox’s Quarters and chaining Gravity Boots without Leapstone.

    • The Players MAY use Wolf stair jumping mechanics to reach otherwise restricted areas.

    • The Players MUST NOT use any shop glitches such as saleable gem duping.

    • Spike Breaker MUST be equipped while going through the spike hallway in Royal Chapel.

    • The Players MUST NOT use potion abuse in order to go through the jewel door in the spike hallway in Royal Chapel.

    • The Players MUST NOT perform any wall or floor clips or zips. This includes, for instance, RSL and stair clipping using Wolf untransform.

    • The Players MUST NOT skip the Gold and Silver Ring puzzle. The Players MUST obtain both the Gold and Silver Rings and wait for the clock room floor to open normally.

    • The Players MUST obtain Holy Glasses before reaching the second castle.

    • The Players MUST NOT skip Richter Belmont using any glitches such as Wolf or Bat skip.

    • The Players MUST NOT skip the second castle relic puzzle using any glitches. The Players MUST obtain all five Vlad relics and wait for the second castle clock room ceiling to open normally.

  • The Players MUST NOT use turbo controllers or controllers that allow pressing opposing directional inputs at the same time (left + right or up + down).

  • The Players MAY use “Save splitting”. That is to say, the Players may wish to save before branching off from their progression paths towards possibly worthless, yet time consuming, relic checks. In the worst case scenario, the Players are allowed to reset and load from their saves in order to avoid the time spent backtracking. Both soft resets [holding START + SELECT] and hard resets are allowed.

  • The Players MUST NOT use save states.

  • The Players MAY choose to restart their match and keep their seed if they both agree and it is reasonably early into their match. Reasons for restarting MAY include, but are not limited to, a particularly bad prologue fight, or a death early in the match. Reasons for restarting MUST NOT include a regrettable routing choice. While Players are encouraged to make their best efforts in overcoming the odds rather than restarting, Referees will recognize that stronger competition is often more fun for the Players and audience, and that may require restarting the match.

  • The Players MUST consult with a Referee in the event of a softlock or emulator crash. Please be advised that there are no known unavoidable crashes using randomized game files on PS1, PS2, or “accurate” emulators, such as the most recent versions of Bizhawk and PSXjin, and that using anything else may result in a Player’s disqualification.

Terms

  • PLAYERS are also known as Participants or Racers.

  • TOURNAMENT OFFICALS are any of the following; Organizers, Restreamers, Commentators and Referees.

  • COMMENTATORS are considered Referees for the matches and are knowledgeable Players that will provide play by play commentary of the matches.

  • RESTREAMERS are persons that will be handling the broadcasting of Player feeds and the matches.

Player Etiquette

  • Players MUST schedule their matches with their opponents and submit a minimum of 2 dates and times.

  • Players MUST submit their chosen dates and times to Tournament Officials approximately 4 to 7 days prior.

  • Once confirmation of the chosen date and time are followed up by Tournament Officials, Players will be notified of when the match will occur.

  • In the the event that a Player cannot meet the set date and time, please contact Tournament Officials ASAP to let them know so rescheduling can be done, advanced notice of at least a day or two is preferred.

  • Players MUST show up approximately 30 mins before the match is to take place for setup. If a Player will be delayed, Tournament Officials MUST be notified ASAP.

  • Failure of any of the above guidelines for scheduling MAY result in match forfeit and/or ejection from the tournament on a per case bases.

  • Players MUST refrain from breaking any of Twitch's Terms of Service (TOS) as this is where matches will be viewed publicly as well as be respectful of all Players and Tournament Officials.

Player Setup

  • All Players will be streaming to an RTMP using a custom server URL.

  • Prior to the match, Players will be given the "Server URL" and a unique "Stream Key" from the Restreamer. This will be case sensitive and Players must make sure to copy and paste them to avoid any issues.

  • All streams from the Players will include only the Game and its Audio.

  • Players MUST mute Discord and microphone audio during races.

  • Players MUST balance their own audio sources beforehand.

  • Game Source MUST be displayed and aligned to the top left of the screen inside OBS.

  • For proper game and emulation settings, refer to the pinned messages in The Long Library Discord server's #Randomizer channel.

  • Players should stream at an appropriate resolution size and bit rate for their own connection.
